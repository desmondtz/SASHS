The Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS) strand at Stand Alone Senior High School within Taguig Integrated School is the gateway for aspiring leaders, communicators, and advocates of social change. Designed for students passionate about history, literature, communication, and the social sciences, HUMSS hones critical thinking, creativity, and interpersonal skills essential for careers in education, journalism, law, public service, and more. With a curriculum that encourages active engagement in societal issues and hands-on experiences like debates, research projects, and community outreach, HUMSS prepares students to make a meaningful impact. Join the HUMSS strand at Taguig Integrated School and turn your passion for people and ideas into a powerful future!
